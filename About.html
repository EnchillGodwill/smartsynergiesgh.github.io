About us

who we are

The Company was established in the year 2020 and was officially registered at the Registrar General's as Smart Synergies with the registration Number Bn120762020.
Smart Synergies is a complete integrated corporate/marketing communications company. The company provides services to a wide range of clients and does business throughout Ghana.
Aside of this, SMART SYNERGIES is also into civil works/construction. Besides our experience as a company, we draw on the strengths, knowledge, skills and experiences from our chain of associates and institutions who are willing to provide the necessary human and technical support when the need arises.
